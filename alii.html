Ka Moʻokūʻauhau Aliʻi

The Royal Lineage — From the Gods to Generation 10

In Hawaiian tradition, there is no separation between mythology and genealogy. The aliʻi traced their lineage continuously and unbroken from the gods to themselves — this was the source of their mana (spiritual power). This is the royal lineage of the Low family, spanning from Kāne (57 BC) through 2,068 years to Generation 10 today.

The names here echo across the entire Polynesian triangle — Hawaii, Aotearoa (New Zealand), and Samoa — because they share the same ancestral roots. Kāne is Tāne in Māori. Laka is Raka. Your ancestors did not just come from one place. They came from everywhere the ocean touches. 🌊
