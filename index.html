Ka Moʻokūʻauhau

The Hawaiian Lineage of the Low Family

This timeline traces the Hawaiian maternal ancestry of Leonard Low Jr.—from Kapokini (circa 1756) in Pololu Valley, Kohala—alongside the paternal lineage of the Low family: Chinese immigrants who became Hawaiian, and the royal Piʻilani bloodline of Maui carried through Rachel Lahela Hulimai Piʻilani. Together these threads weave the full heritage of Generation 10. Portuguese and Polish lineages await future chapters.
