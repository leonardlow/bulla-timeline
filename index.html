Your Place in History

This is the story of the Low family—a lineage spanning 255 years, from Kapokini in Pololu Valley (1756) to you today. Whether your connection to this family came through birth, adoption, marriage, or love, this legacy is yours. Our ancestors witnessed the rise and transformation of the Hawaiian Kingdom, the shaping of a nation, and moments large and small that wove our family into the fabric of history. You carry that legacy forward.
